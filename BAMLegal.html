Short version: We collect only what we need to run the service. We don't sell your data. You can request deletion at any time. We comply with UK GDPR.

1. Introduction

Synthia.ai ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Our lawful bases for processing your data are: contractual necessity (to deliver the service), legitimate interests (to operate and improve the platform), and legal obligation (compliance with applicable law).

2. Data We Collect

Account Data

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Password (stored as a salted hash — never in plain text)
  • Account creation date and last login
  • Age confirmation (boolean — we do not store your date of birth)

Billing Data

  • Credit balance and transaction history
  • Stripe payment intent IDs (for reconciliation)
  • We do not store card numbers, CVVs, or full payment details — these are handled exclusively by Stripe

Usage Data

  • Generation logs: model used, cost, timestamp
  • We do not store your prompts or generated images on our servers long-term

Technical Data

  • IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
  • Browser type and device type
  • Session tokens

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service — authenticate you, process generations, manage your credit balance
  • To process payments — via Stripe, to top up your account
  • To communicate with you — account notifications, policy updates, support responses
  • For security — fraud detection, abuse prevention, account integrity
  • Legal compliance — to meet our obligations under UK law

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to train AI models.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on EU-region servers. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+).

Access to your data is restricted to authorised personnel only. We implement industry-standard security measures including row-level security policies on our database.

Despite our best efforts, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by UK GDPR.

5. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third parties only as necessary to deliver the service:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Their privacy policy: stripe.com/gb/privacy
  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting
  • Vercel — platform hosting and edge functions
  • AI model providers (WaveSpeed, EvoLink, LaoZhang) — your prompts and reference images are transmitted to these providers to process generations. We recommend reviewing their terms if you have concerns about specific input data

We do not share your personal data (name, email, billing information) with AI model providers.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained for the lifetime of your account plus 90 days after deletion
  • Transaction records — retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Generation logs — retained for 90 days then deleted
  • IP logs — retained for 30 days

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention obligations
  • Right to restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@synthia.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings but this will prevent you from logging in.

Acceptable Use Policy

Synthia.ai · Last updated: April 2026 · Applies to all users

Important: Violation of this policy may result in immediate account termination without refund. Certain violations will be reported to relevant authorities.

1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules governing what content may and may not be generated using Synthia.ai. It applies to all users without exception and forms part of our Terms and Conditions.

Synthia.ai provides powerful AI generation capabilities. With that power comes responsibility. These rules exist to protect individuals, protect you legally, and maintain the integrity of the platform.

2. Age Requirement

You must be 18 or older to use Synthia.ai. This applies to all features including those unrelated to adult content. By using the platform you confirm you are 18 or older.

Any attempt to circumvent age verification or create accounts on behalf of minors will result in permanent account termination and may be reported to appropriate authorities.

3. Absolutely Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited under all circumstances. Generating, attempting to generate, or distributing this content will result in immediate termination and may result in reports to law enforcement:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) — any sexual or exploitative content involving individuals under 18, whether real or AI-generated. This is illegal under UK law (Sexual Offences Act 2003, Protection of Children Act 1978) and will be reported to the National Crime Agency (NCA) and Internet Watch Foundation (IWF)
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery of real, identifiable individuals ("deepfake pornography") — illegal under the Online Safety Act 2023
  • Content designed to facilitate real-world violence — instructions, planning material, or incitement
  • Terrorism or extremist content — material glorifying, promoting, or supporting terrorist organisations
  • Content targeting specific real individuals for harassment, defamation, or harm

4. Adult / NSFW Content Rules

Synthia.ai includes NSFW generation capabilities. Access to these features is subject to the following rules:

  • All subjects must be depicted as clearly adult (18+) in appearance and context
  • Do not generate content that ambiguously depicts or could be interpreted as depicting minors — if in doubt, do not generate it
  • NSFW content generated on Synthia.ai is for personal or commercial use within legal limits of your jurisdiction
  • You are responsible for ensuring any NSFW content you publish or distribute complies with the platform and laws of the jurisdiction where it is published
  • Do not attempt to use NSFW features to generate real persons without their consent

Zero tolerance: Any content that appears to depict a minor in a sexual context — regardless of claimed intent — will result in immediate termination and a mandatory report to the IWF and NCA.

5. Real People

Generating realistic images or videos of real, identifiable individuals requires care:

  • Public figures — satirical or clearly non-realistic depictions may be acceptable; realistic depictions designed to deceive or defame are not
  • Private individuals — do not generate realistic images of private individuals without their explicit consent
  • Intimate content — never generate intimate or sexual content featuring real identifiable individuals under any circumstances
  • Synthetic personas (AI influencers, fictional characters) — permitted provided they are clearly not representations of real, identifiable individuals

6. Commercial Use

You may use Synthia.ai outputs for commercial purposes including:

  • Social media content and marketing
  • AI influencer personas (subject to platform rules of the publishing platform)
  • Product imagery and promotional material
  • Content subscription platforms, subject to those platforms' own age verification and content rules

You are solely responsible for ensuring your commercial use complies with the rules of any third-party platform where you publish content, including age verification obligations under the Online Safety Act 2023 for adult content platforms.

7. Enforcement

We reserve the right to:

  • Suspend or terminate accounts at our sole discretion for policy violations
  • Remove generated content that violates this policy
  • Report illegal content to relevant authorities including the NCA, IWF, and law enforcement
  • Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

Account termination for AUP violations does not entitle you to a refund of remaining credits.

8. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of content or activity on Synthia.ai that violates this policy, please report it immediately.

Report abuse: abuse@synthia.ai

Reports are reviewed promptly. We take all reports seriously.

For child safety concerns you can also report directly to the Internet Watch Foundation at iwf.org.uk
