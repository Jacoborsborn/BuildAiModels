BuildAIModels is a professional AI generation studio for creating hyper-realistic AI influencer personas. Generate consistent, studio-quality images and videos for Instagram, TikTok, Fanvue, and other platforms — without any photography or design skills.

No. Credits never expire. Top up when you need to and use them at your own pace. There are no monthly fees or subscription commitments.
Can I use the content commercially? +
Yes. All content you generate is yours to use commercially — social media, subscription platforms, brand deals, marketing. You own the outputs. See our Terms & Conditions for full details.
How realistic is the output? +
Extremely. We use the latest frontier models including Nano Banana Pro (Google Gemini-based) and Seedream 5.0 Lite at 4K resolution. With proper prompting and reference images, outputs are indistinguishable from professional photography.
Is there an age restriction? +
Yes. You must be 18 or older to use BuildAIModels. This applies to all features including those unrelated to adult content. See our Acceptable Use Policy for full rules.

If a generation fails due to a platform error on our side, the credit cost is automatically returned to your account. You are never charged for failed generations caused by our systems.
